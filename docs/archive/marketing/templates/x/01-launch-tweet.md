# X (Twitter) template — launch-day tweet

> **When**: Week 3 launch day (~2026-05-13)
> **Format**: short, image-led, with link in reply
> **Cadence**: one launch tweet, then nothing for 24h

---

## Tweet structure

**Image-led, short body, no thread.** X rewards a hook + visual + brevity.

```
Three months solo. Just shipped Unit — a 3-second gym logger for
intermediate lifters who hate spreadsheets.

Free core logging. No AI coach. No social feed.

[Image: hero screenshot OR 5-second video clip of logging a set]
```

**Reply (within 30 seconds of posting)**:

```
App Store: [App Store URL]
```

---

## Image / video options

Pick one — don't post the launch tweet without media.

1. **Hero screenshot** (Unit's first App Store screenshot — the "log a set in 3 seconds" claim with device frame). Pulls from `docs/app-store-copy.md`.
2. **5-8 second video clip** (vertical or square — X compresses both fine). Tradesman demo from `templates/tiktok-ig/01-tradesman-demo.md` cut to length.
3. **Side-by-side notebook+Unit comparison** (still). Cut from the notebook-vs-Unit format.

Don't post links without media — they get suppressed by the algorithm.

---

## Why X-specific framing matters

X audience for indie iOS = devs, founders, prosumer iOS users. Different from TikTok/IG audience.

What works on X (per `research/viral-patterns-2026-04-29.md` §3 Tony Dinh playbook):
- Tech-stack one-liner ("SwiftUI + SwiftData + RevenueCat") — devs upvote when they recognize the stack
- Numbers in body, not just images
- Short-thread or single-tweet, never carousel
- Reply with link — X downranks tweets with links in body

What doesn't work on X:
- Long threads (skip thread format for the launch — use it for the W4 retro tweet)
- Hashtags (X de-prioritizes hashtagged tweets)
- "Hey friends, today I'm launching…" — too casual

---

## Posting time

**Best windows for X (indie iOS audience)**:
- Tuesday or Wednesday, 9-11am ET
- Avoid Sundays (low traffic) and Friday afternoons

Match the day to your Reddit launch post — same day works (X and Reddit audiences barely overlap, so cross-pollination doesn't matter).

---

## Pre-post checklist

- [ ] Image or video attached
- [ ] Body ≤ 280 characters (X limit)
- [ ] No more than 1 hashtag (better: zero)
- [ ] Link prepared for reply (not in main tweet)
- [ ] Pinned to profile after posting
- [ ] Reply with link within 30 seconds of posting
- [ ] Block 2 hours after posting for replies + DMs

---

## Post-time discipline

| Time | Action |
|---|---|
| **T+0** | Post tweet. Reply with App Store link within 30s. Pin to profile. |
| **T+0 to T+2h** | Reply to every quote-tweet, mention, DM. Especially first hour. |
| **T+24h** | Log impressions, likes, replies, profile visits, App Store taps. |

---

## Follow-up tweets (W3-W4)

After the launch tweet, drop one of each over the next 2 weeks (NOT same day):
- A 5-tweet thread covering the 3 months of building (`templates/x/02-build-in-public-thread.md`)
- A pricing-philosophy one-liner (`templates/x/03-pricing-philosophy.md`)
- 2-3 "screen demo" tweets — same content as TikTok tradesman demos, repurposed for X (8-15s clips)

---

## Don't do

- ❌ Link in the main tweet body (suppressed)
- ❌ Hashtags (de-prioritized in 2025-2026)
- ❌ Generic "I'm launching today!" energy
- ❌ Long thread on launch day — single tweet hits harder
- ❌ Tag random influencers asking for boosts
- ❌ Engagement bait ("RT if you've ever quit Hevy")
