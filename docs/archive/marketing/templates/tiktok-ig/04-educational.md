# TikTok/IG template — Educational micro-lesson

> **Cadence**: 1 every 2 weeks.
> **Length**: 30-60s vertical.
> **Source**: Source A (DIY talking-head) or Source C (ElevenLabs voiceover over screen).

---

## Structure

| Time | Action |
|---|---|
| **0-3s** | Contrarian one-liner. Must hook. |
| **3-50s** | 3-step explanation with screen visuals |
| **50-60s** | Closing — *"Unit shows your last session. Never tells you what to lift. Free on App Store."* |

---

## Topic options (rotate)

| # | Topic | Hook |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Why ghost values destroy progression engines | *"Every progression engine is broken. Here's the math."* |
| 2 | Why "AI coach" recommendations cost you reps | *"The 'AI coach' in your gym app is making you weaker."* |
| 3 | The math of "seconds per set logged" (your North Star metric) | *"3 seconds per set. 50 sets per week. That's 150 seconds. That's your tracker tax."* |
| 4 | Why notebooks beat every gym tracker until 2024 | *"Pen and paper beats every gym app. Here's why that's the bug, not the feature."* |
| 5 | What "intermediate-to-advanced lifter" actually means in app design | *"There's a moment in lifting when 'gym apps' stop helping and start slowing you down."* |
| 6 | Why bodyweight movements should NOT show "0 kg" | *"If your gym app shows '0 kg' for a pullup, it doesn't get your sport."* |
| 7 | Why the Apple Watch + iPhone combo for logging is overrated | *"You don't need a watch. You need 3 taps."* |
| 8 | Why Hevy / Strong / Liftosaur all lost the plot the same way | *"Three apps. Three different bloat paths. Same root cause."* |

## Body structure (3 steps)

For topic #1 ("ghost values vs progression engines"):

```
Step 1 — what progression engines try to do:
"Every smart gym app tries to predict your next lift. Hit your reps?
Add 2.5kg. Miss? Repeat. Miss 3 times? Deload 10%."

Step 2 — why that breaks under fatigue:
"On a real Tuesday at 9pm after work, you don't follow the engine.
You feel out the warmup, decide if you've got it, and adjust. The
engine doesn't know any of that. It just knows the algorithm."

Step 3 — what ghost values do instead:
"Show last session's weight + reps as ghost text. Athlete decides.
One tap to confirm, one to adjust. The engine doesn't need to
predict — the lifter already knows."

Closing:
"Unit shows your last session. Never tells you what to lift. Free on
App Store."
```

Adapt the 3-step structure for whichever topic you pick.

---

## Visuals

For each step, **show the screen**. Either:
- Screen-record of Unit demonstrating the concept (preferred)
- Side-by-side: Unit's UI vs competitor's UI
- Text overlay over screen recording

Don't talk over a static image. The screen is the unfakeable artifact.

---

## Voiceover

You + camera (talking-head) OR ElevenLabs voice clone over screen-only footage. **Calm, dry, no music.**

Per `PRODUCT.md` brand voice: peer-to-peer, not lecturing. Talk like you're explaining to one lifter friend, not to a class.

---

## Closing

End with the same line every time (consistency builds recall):
> *"Unit shows your last session. Never tells you what to lift. Free on App Store."*

OR a topic-specific closing that links back to the same thesis:
> *"Athlete autonomy is the feature. Free on App Store."*

---

## Why this format works

Per `research/viral-patterns-2026-04-29.md` §2b:
> Educational content has long tail on TikTok — clips can resurface months later. Hooks must be contrarian (not "let me walk you through our features" — that's death).

Educational format builds your authority — viewers who don't install today come back when their current app frustrates them.

## Why we cap at every 2 weeks

Educational content is heavy. One per week feels like a podcast you didn't ask for. Every 2 weeks keeps the audience curious.

---

## Recording checklist

- [ ] Topic chosen (rotate from list, don't repeat within 8 weeks)
- [ ] 3 step structure mapped
- [ ] Hook (first 3s) is contrarian, not corporate
- [ ] Screen-record of Unit demonstrating each step
- [ ] No "let me walk you through" framing

## Post-production

- [ ] Submagic captions
- [ ] CapCut: cut every 5-8s to keep momentum
- [ ] Timer overlays where relevant
- [ ] Closing screen
- [ ] 1080×1920 vertical

---

## Don't do

- ❌ Corporate "let me walk you through our features" framing
- ❌ Generic "fitness tips" that don't tie to Unit
- ❌ Lecturing tone — talk peer-to-peer
- ❌ Skip the screen visuals — talking-only educationals don't perform
