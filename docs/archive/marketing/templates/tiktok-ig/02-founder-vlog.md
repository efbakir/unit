# TikTok/IG template — Founder vlog

> **Cadence**: 1/week.
> **Length**: 30-45s vertical.
> **Source**: Source A (DIY) primary. UGC creators can do a variant.

---

## Structure

| Time | Action |
|---|---|
| **0-3s** | You talking to camera (gym OR desk). Frustration-led hook. |
| **3-25s** | 2-3 specific complaints about competitor apps. Be specific — "the set log has 4 taps before haptic", not "it's slow." |
| **25-40s** | 1 specific thing Unit does instead. **Show it on screen.** |
| **40-45s** | Engagement question or close. *"Show me your tracker setup in comments."* |

---

## Hook variants (first 3 seconds)

**A** (frustration-led):
> *"I quit Hevy 4 months ago because it felt like a spreadsheet under fatigue."*

**B** (specific moment):
> *"Last Tuesday, my 5x5 set timer hit 90 seconds and I still hadn't logged the previous set."*

**C** (direct opinion):
> *"Every gym app in 2026 is bloated. Here's what should have been removed."*

**D** (build-in-public framing):
> *"3 months solo. I built the gym logger I actually wanted to use."*

---

## Body (the 2-3 specific complaints — pick from this list, rotate weekly)

| Complaint | Specific |
|---|---|
| Hevy set log | "4 taps before the haptic. Set selector → weight → reps → done. That's 3 too many." |
| Strong onboarding | "Asks me to pick from 47 templates before I can log a set." |
| Liftosaur programming | "Brilliant for programmers. Slow if you just want to log." |
| AI coach apps (generic) | "Tells me what to lift. I already know what to lift." |
| Notes app workaround | "Yes I tracked sets in Notes for 18 months. It's faster than every app I tried." |
| Gym social feeds | "Strava for lifters. I don't want to share my squats." |

---

## "What Unit does instead" (pick one per video, rotate)

- **Last time**: "Last session's weight and reps are right there. One tap to confirm."
- **Auto-timer**: "Rest timer auto-starts on 'Done'. Lives on the Lock Screen and Dynamic Island."
- **No coach**: "Shows your last session. Never tells you what to lift next."
- **3-tap log**: "Weight, reps, done. 2.4 seconds under fatigue."
- **Local-first**: "No account. No cloud. Works in basement gyms."

---

## Closing

End with one of:
- *"Show me your tracker setup in comments."* (engagement)
- *"Link in bio. Free on App Store."*
- Silence, close on app icon

---

## Recording checklist

- [ ] Phone on tripod
- [ ] Quiet space OR ambient gym noise (both work)
- [ ] Window light or basic gym lighting
- [ ] Optional: lavalier mic ($15)
- [ ] One take, two max — first-thought is most authentic
- [ ] No "hi guys", no "today I'm going to talk about"

---

## Post-production

- [ ] Submagic auto-captions
- [ ] Submagic clip-selection if you recorded long (>60s) — pick strongest 30-45s. Add Magic Clips addon only if raw >5min.
- [ ] CapCut: insert screen-record clip(s) of the app for the "what Unit does" section
- [ ] No music with lyrics — silent or subtle ambient bed (CapCut royalty-free)
- [ ] 1080×1920 vertical, MP4

## Why this format works

Per `research/viral-patterns-2026-04-29.md` §2c — the **Tony Dinh playbook**: build → tweet/post the actual screen → repeat for months until one demo lands. Audience trusts the human + the screen demo combo.

For an audience-of-zero indie iOS dev, founder vlog is the strongest format. The credible voice + visible demo is the unfakeable combination.

## Caveats

- **No founder-vlog without showing the screen.** Per research: "Founder-face talking-head with no demo (works for huge accounts; for <5k followers you need the screen on-screen)."
- Keep it dry — `PRODUCT.md` brand voice: *calm, expert, honest*. No "OMG you guys", no excessive enthusiasm, no "huge announcement".
- **Brand-cohesive criticism.** Don't mock competitors personally. Critique features, not founders.

## Don't do

- ❌ "Hi guys" / "today I'm going to" intro — kills 3s retention
- ❌ Music with lyrics
- ❌ Cuts every 0.5s — fitness audience finds this exhausting
- ❌ Quote-overs of Naval/Levels/founder wisdom (research §4 anti-pattern)
- ❌ Founder-vlog without showing the app screen

## Variant for UGC creators (Source B)

If a UGC creator does a founder-vlog-style video, the brief is identical except:
- Replace "I quit Hevy" with "I switched to Unit from Hevy"
- They speak in their own words, not yours
- Keep the screen-on-screen rule
