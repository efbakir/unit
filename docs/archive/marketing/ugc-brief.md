## Brand context (paste at the top of the Billo brief)

Unit is a minimalist iOS gym logger for **intermediate-to-advanced lifters** (1-10+ years training). The product wins by *removing*, not adding — no AI coach, no social feed, no progression engine. The North Star is "log a set in under 3 seconds, one-handed, sweaty."

Brand voice: **calm, expert, honest**. No hype, no motivational copy, no "transform your training" energy. Talk to lifters as peers who already know the work.

---

## Casting criterion (the most important section)

✅ **Cast for**:
- Real intermediate-to-advanced lifter (1+ years training under their belt)
- Real gym setting — commercial gym, garage gym, university gym
- Comfortable narrating in their own voice
- Looks like Unit's customer: serious about lifting, not a content-first creator
- Any gender, any age, any background — the only filter is *do they actually train?*

❌ **Don't cast**:
- Models, beauty/aesthetic-app creators, lifestyle influencers
- Non-lifters in gymwear (the "wears gym clothes for content" demographic)
- Anyone whose recent UGC is clearly affiliate / hype-driven
- Aesthetic-gym chains (commercial-gym is fine; "Equinox studio" content reads as ad)
- Anyone who would describe their own content as "fitspo"

The cast is for **authentic lifter signal**, not demographic clickbait. Many of the best UGC creators in this niche are women lifters, men lifters, older lifters — what unifies them is they look like they actually train, not like they're auditioning.

---

## Format spec

**Length**: 30-60 seconds, vertical (9:16, 1080×1920).

**Composition**:
- Show the app working (their phone, their hands, their plates loaded behind them)
- At least one shot of the screen of the app while they tap through a set
- Their face on camera at some point (doesn't have to be the whole video)

**Voice**:
- Their own, in their own words
- Calm, conversational, not announcing
- No music with lyrics
- Optional: ambient gym noise

**No hype**:
- Not "this app changed my training"
- Not "I'm so excited to share this"
- Not "you NEED to try this"
- Yes "I started using this. It's faster than my notes app."

---

## Hooks (creator picks one)

**Hook 1 — Tradesman demo**:
> "I'm logging a set right now. One-handed. Sweaty. Watch how fast this is." [shows screen, taps through one set with timer overlay]

**Hook 2 — Frustration before solution**:
> "I quit [Hevy / Strong / Notion] last month. The set log was too slow under fatigue. Now I use this." [shows demo]

**Hook 3 — Speed proof**:
> "Notebook took me 8 seconds to log a set. This app takes me 2." [side-by-side or split-screen demo]

Don't write the script for them — let them adapt it to their own voice. The hooks above are starting points.

---

## CTA

End the video with one of:
- *"I'm using Unit. Free on App Store."*
- *"Link in bio if you want to try it."*
- Silence — just close on the app icon

❌ Never:
- *"Use my code XX for…"*
- *"Smash that download button"*
- *"Drop a 🔥 if you train"*
- Any affiliate-marketing energy

---

## Submission requirements

Per Billo / Insense / JoinBrands defaults:
- 1080p vertical raw video
- No watermark from the platform
- 1 free revision included if needed
- Usage rights: paid social, organic social, App Store preview eligible
