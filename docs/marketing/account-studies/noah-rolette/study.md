# noah.rolette — the iPhone-only creator playbook

Studied 2026-06-11. Sources: his 8-slide carousel (screenshots in this folder), web metadata.

## 1. Who / numbers

- 634K followers, 242 posts, bio "Hobby collector". Founder of @rawdawgclub (run club) and @nocco.usa.
- Claims a $30k+/month platform built entirely from an iPhone — no camera crew, native tools only.
- Content spine: his life (surfing, horses, Kilimanjaro, freediving) with businesses placed inside it, not the other way round.

## 2. The 7 tips, transcribed from the carousel

| # | Slide | Claim |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | The hook | Nothing matters more than the first 3 seconds. Don't edit chronologically — pull the most engaging moment to the front as a sneak peek that breaks the doomscroll. |
| 2 | Reels vs carousels | Reels reach non-followers → growth. Trial reels add reach (shown only to non-followers). Carousels serve the existing audience → depth, life-inside view, subtle product placement. |
| 3 | How to edit | Editing is the "work" that kills consistency. Instagram's **Edits** app: paste a link to any video whose style you like → it rebuilds that template with your content. He claims Edits-made ("3 edits") uploads get a distribution boost; even externally edited video gets reuploaded through Edits. |
| 4 | Story / journey | Challenges (75 Hard, @higherupwellness challenge) had the biggest impact he's seen. A journey forces daily posting, shows visible results, trains speak-to-camera skill. |
| 5 | Volume over perfection | Minimum 1 post/day; 2-4/day across all platforms when growth is the goal. Not every video is a banger; volume is the only way to get better. |
| 6 | Trendy vs original | Trends pump numbers but build no brand — no meat. Use trends as a net for new eyes; original content is why the new eyes stay. |
| 7 | Authentic, not facade | Don't LARP a life — 90% of viewers are in the same place; relatability wins. iPhone footage that feels like a friend's group-chat video beats high production. |

## 3. Copy for Unit

| Mechanic | Where it lands |
|---|---|
| Non-chronological editing — most engaging frame first | Tightens the existing hook rule in `tiktok-ig.md`. For Unit clips: open on the set already logged ("2.4s"), then context. |
| Carousels as a second format | New. Current strategy is reels-only. Carousels are cheap for Unit: app screenshots + training-log pages + build-in-public slides. Fills posting days without filming. |
| Trial reels | New, free reach. Turn on for every reel. |
| Edits app pipeline | Test it. Platform-native templates, not AI slop — compliant. Could replace part of the CapCut step and cut per-clip edit time. The "Edits uploads get boosted" claim is unverified — A/B it. |
| Journey spine | Maps to build-in-public, already a Unit format. Strongest version: founder trains with his own app in public — "12 weeks, every set logged in Unit" as a personal challenge. Marketing surface only; streaks stay banned in-app (PRODUCT.md). |
| iPhone-only authenticity | Validates the existing DIY pipeline. Stop treating low production as a gap to apologize for; it is the format. |

## 4. Decisions and standing filters

| Item | Status |
|---|---|
| 1-4 posts/day | **Decided 2026-06-11 — adopted.** Founder call: app is built, marketing mode replaces build mode, the 3-hrs/month budget (`cadence.md`) was a build-mode assumption and no longer binds. Lead with free. Production recipe pending — reels alone cannot fill 30-120 slots/month; the mix is reels + carousels + raw journey clips + Edits templates. `cadence.md` / `content-engine.md` cascade once the mix is locked. |
| Multiple accounts | **Adopted with a hard line.** Differentiated identities only — each account has its own content angle (this is noah's own structure: personal + @rawdawgclub + @nocco.usa). Clone accounts reposting the same clip are out: Instagram dedupes identical content across accounts and suppresses unoriginal reposts, and it is the detectable-automation pattern `anti-patterns.md` exists to block. |
| Ride trends | Standing filter, unchanged: trending formats/sounds only when they survive the calm brand-voice test (PRODUCT.md). Never force a meme voice. |

## 5. Skip

- His niche is lifestyle-as-business; Unit is a tool. Copy the *mechanics*, not the lifestyle framing — Unit content still anchors on the product moment (the log, the timer), per `tiktok-ig.md`.
- No clip lifting, no reusing his footage or face (`anti-patterns.md`).
